Sleep Duration Recommendations

  • School-aged children (6-13 years)-9-11 hours
  • Teenagers (14-17 years)-8-10 hours
  • Young adults (18-25 years)-7-9 hours
  • Adults (26-64 years)-7-9 hours
  • Older adults (65+ years)-7-8 hours
